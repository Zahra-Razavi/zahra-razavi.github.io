Lendeck — FinTech platform, designed a 3-sided lending dashboard

A dashboard for SMBs to manage IVR, extensions, call logs, and analytics. I led UX from discovery to delivery, aligning stakeholders and shipping a Polaris-consistent dashboard.

  • Role: Product Designer & PM
  • Timeline: 5 months
  • Team: 1 PM, 2 FE devs, 1 BE dev
  • Platform: Web (responsive)

Problem

Customers struggled to configure IVR and manage call flows. High setup friction caused drop-offs in onboarding and support tickets.

Goals & Constraints

  • Reduce onboarding drop-off
  • Enable non-technical admins to configure IVR
  • Stay within existing tech stack and Polaris guidelines
  • Deliver MVP in 10 weeks, iterate post-launch

Process

  1. User interviews and task analyses with 8 admins
  2. Flow mapping for IVR, extensions, and call routing
  3. Low-fi → hi-fi prototypes; hallway tests
  4. Design QA and release checklist

Solution

A guided setup with clear hierarchy, previewable IVR, and analytics surfaced at the right moments. Accessible, responsive layout with consistent components.

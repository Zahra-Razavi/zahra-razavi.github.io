Lendeck, 3-sided lending platform

Case Study · Product Design

Lendeck is a B2B FinTech lending platform designed to streamline the loan lifecycle between brokers, lenders, and merchants. I led the end-to-end UX/UI design for this multi-user dashboard, optimizing the deal submission, underwriting, and funding processes through automation and AI-assisted workflows.

The redesigned dashboard reduced delivery time by 50% and enabled smarter document orchestration aligned with KYC/AML and underwriting standards.

Problem

Traditional SME lending platforms are often fragmented, with inconsistent communication between brokers and lenders, manual document processing, and long underwriting times. Lendeck needed a unified and transparent dashboard that would simplify deal submission, reduce back-and-forth interactions, and improve speed and compliance in the underwriting process.

Our Goals

  • Create a single, intuitive platform for brokers, lenders, and merchants.
  • Automate manual data collection and verification.
  • Enable transparent communication between all parties.
  • Reduce approval and funding turnaround time.
  • Ensure compliance with KYC/AML and risk-assessment frameworks.

Discovery

To fully understand the ecosystem, I conducted research and interviews with all three user types to uncover workflow challenges and pain points in the lending process.

Actions

  • Mapped the end-to-end lending process to identify inefficiencies.
  • Defined user roles and responsibilities across brokers, lenders, and merchants.
  • Validated assumptions through interviews and testing.

FinTech and Lending Background Research

I analyzed the lending industry’s digital transformation, focusing on trends in automation, open banking APIs, and AI-based risk evaluation. This helped shape the product vision toward a smarter, compliance-first lending experience.

Interview Stakeholders

Stakeholders (product, legal, risk, and tech teams) shared insights about operational bottlenecks, compliance rules, and strategic goals for automation and transparency.

Interview Users of All 3 Sides

  • Brokers struggled with fragmented document workflows and slow lender responses.
  • Lenders faced inconsistent data quality and manual risk assessments.
  • Merchants lacked visibility into their loan status and document requirements.

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed key competitors — BanadaExchange.com, CashAdvance.com, and other potential B2B lending tools — to benchmark usability, feature sets, and communication flows. This informed our differentiation strategy focusing on automation, transparency, and human-centered design.

Insights

Users desired transparency, automation, and trust. Speed of decision-making and clarity of communication were crucial. The insight led to designing a dashboard that visualizes deal progress, centralizes communication, and automates document handling.

User Journey

The journey visualized handoffs across brokers, lenders, and merchants, surfacing where automation and guidance could reduce friction.

User Pain

Classic

Manual document uploads, unclear loan progress, delayed responses, and repetitive data entry.

Competitive

Competitors lacked unified dashboards and clear progress visibility; users often switched between platforms or emails to track one deal.

Site Map

User Flow

Each user type’s flow was optimized to reduce steps and cognitive load. Brokers can submit deals in under three minutes; lenders review with automated insights; merchants upload documents with guided prompts.

Fast Prototype & Test

Low-fidelity prototypes were created in Figma and tested with eight participants across roles. Usability tests after each iteration ensured smoother navigation and reduced confusion in document management.

UI/UX Design

The interface combines a modern, trustworthy aesthetic with FinTech clarity—using a clean layout, contrast-driven hierarchy, and contextual microinteractions. I applied atomic design principles to maintain consistency across modules and scalable components.

Outcomes & Impact

The redesigned Lendeck dashboard transformed the lending process from a fragmented, email-heavy workflow into a fast, transparent digital experience for brokers, lenders, and merchants. Through multiple testing rounds and design iterations, we achieved measurable results:

  • ~50% faster deal processing thanks to automated underwriting and document workflows.
  • 70% reduction in communication delays through built-in real-time updates and document status tracking.
  • Improved data accuracy as centralized data entry and automated KYC/AML validation reduced human errors.
  • Higher user satisfaction with brokers and lenders rating usability and clarity 4.6/5 on average.
  • Better alignment across teams so compliance, underwriting, and product could collaborate in one unified space.

This outcome validated the design direction: a platform that accelerates lending operations and builds trust through visibility and ease of use — setting a new standard for digital lending experiences in the SME space.

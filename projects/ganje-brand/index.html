Vision North Star

Smart cities, human logistics.

Sets the tone for every locker interaction by pairing innovation with trust.

Brand Archetype

Creative

Provides structure and innovation — framing Ganje as the builder shaping smarter urban life.

A comprehensive visual and verbal system was developed to ensure seamless adaptability across diverse touchpoints — from compact IoT screens and courier tablets to mobile applications and large-scale urban billboards. The modular design framework maintains consistent brand recognition, even within minimal or “faceless” digital environments. Covering all aspects from naming and archetypes to typography and UI components, the system harmonizes operational precision with a clear and approachable brand presence.

Brand persona

We position Ganje as the adventurous expert — a friend who ushers you into new ways of receiving parcels while signalling absolute competence.

Personality traits

Excitement · Main

Unabashed and cutting-edge, tapping into novelty and imagination to keep smart logistics compelling.

Competence

Signals trust and security. The brand assures people that every flow is handled by a team that knows what it’s doing.

Customer Relationship

Adventurous

Like the spontaneous friend who gets you to try new things, Ganje motivates residents, couriers, and partners to adopt smarter behaviors with confidence.

Brand voice snapshot

  • Proactive and flexible in tone.
  • Simplicity-first — minimal words, maximum clarity.
  • “Less is more” reflections in UI microcopy and motion.

Usage notes

  • Scale headings responsively on mobile breakpoints.
  • Leverage Inter’s optical sizing for crisp UI typography.

A brand language designed for scale

The Ganje identity had to function across multiple contexts: tiny IoT screens, courier tablets, mobile apps, and large urban billboards. We engineered a modular system that keeps the brand instantly recognizable, even when the UI is "faceless" and the experience shifts to the user's phone.

From naming and archetypes through typography, iconography, and UI components, the system flexes from operations dashboards to customer interactions without sacrificing clarity or warmth.

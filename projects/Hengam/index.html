Case Study · Product Design

Notify Me! - A Shopify App

Role

Solo Product Designer

Timeline

Jan 2022 – Jun 2022

Scope

User research · UX · UI

Tools & Resources

Problem

1. Merchants could not customize push or SMS notifications, creating a backlog of support tickets for manual adjustments. Email customization existed but generated heavy support volume due to limited guidance.

2. Only basic email domain configuration was available. Advanced setups required support intervention, causing friction and confusion during onboarding.

Discovery

Qualitative

  • User behaviour analysis: 20 user session recordings reviewed Hotjar Lucky Orange
  • User feedback synthesized from live chat support Intercom

Quantitative

  • Monthly churn rate tracking
  • Onboarding drop-off analysis across the activation funnel

Notification user flow

High-level steps merchants follow to configure and preview push/SMS notifications, including template selection, channel toggles, and scheduling.

Email setup user flow

Guided experience for domain verification, DNS validation, and template customization to reduce onboarding support requests.

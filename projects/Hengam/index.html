Problem

Merchants struggled to customize critical reminder and preorder confirmation notifications across push and SMS. While email templates were editable, high-volume support tickets highlighted frustration with inconsistent messaging and lack of control.

The product team introduced push notifications to meet demand, but merchants also needed a cohesive way to configure the push widget appearance and copyâ€”without relying on support or developer assistance.

Discovery

Qualitative

  • User behaviour analysis

    20 user session recordings surfaced friction in locating push settings and understanding template hierarchy.

  • Heatmap insights

    Scroll-depth heatmaps showed merchants abandoning customization mid-flow due to preview visibility issues.

  • Chat support feedback

    Support transcripts revealed repeat requests for consistent tone across channels and for dynamic variables in push copy.

Quantitative

  • Churn Rate

    Merchants without multi-channel customization churned 22% faster within the first 60 days.

  • Onboarding Drop-off

    17% of new installs disengaged during onboarding when prompted to accept default notifications.
